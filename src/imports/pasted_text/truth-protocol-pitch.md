TRUTH PROTOCOL

Complete Story-Focused Pitch Deck

Slide 1 — Title

TRUTH PROTOCOL

Action / Adventure / Conspiracy / Military Thriller

Written by
Mike Surick

A man accidentally learns the most dangerous truths in human history. Now, the powers that be will stop at nothing to find him.

Slide 2 — The Truth Protocol

The Truth Protocol is a classified program designed to reveal humanity’s deepest secrets to all who wish to know them, but only in the moments prior death by medically assisted euthanasia.

Artificial Intelligence Systems International (AISI) is responsible for facilitating the program where articipants are injected with a powerful neurotropic drug and connected to an advanced neural AI interface before being exposed to Truth Data — a hidden archive of suppressed intelligence, secret programs, and manipulated historical events.

The purpose of the program is simple:

Allow the truth to be seen… and then die with the witness.

Until one mistake changes everything.

Slide 3 — Michael Riley

Michael R. Riley

Decorated Special Forces veteran.
Elite tactical operator.

After decades of combat deployments, Riley is trying to live a quiet life with his wife Michele and their daughter Lauren.

But Riley is about to become the most dangerous liability the Truth Protocol has ever faced.

Slide 4 — The Accident

After a minor car accident, Riley is hospitalized and mistakenly identified as a volunteer for the Truth Protocol.

Before anyone realizes the mistake, Riley is injected with the program’s cognitive compound and connected to the neural interface.

For the first time in history the entire truth data archive is shown to the wrong person.

Slide 5 — They Try to Kill Him

Program operatives quickly realize the catastrophic mistake.

Riley was never meant to see the data.

Their solution is immediate.

Eliminate the witness.

But Riley escapes the facility with Michele.

Slide 6 — The Manhunt

Riley and Michele go on the run as a covert operation is launched to hunt him down.

AISI brings in rogue CIA SOG Operator Jack Turner to help find Riley.

The forces pursuing him include:

AISI Corporate Security Contractors
CIA Special Operations Group (SOG) assets
Corporate black-site security teams
 

The goal: Kill or capture Riley before he can tell anyone what he saw.

 

Slide 7 — The Ambush

During a brutal roadside ambush, Riley and Michele fight off multiple SOG Operators.

The firefight leaves several operatives dead.

But Michele is fatally wounded.

In her final moments, she makes Riley promise to protect their daughter Lauren.

Slide 8 — Riley Disappears

Devastated and hunted, Riley reconnects with his former Special Forces brothers who trust him and know how to fight a war that officially doesn’t exist.

 

Slide 9 — They Take His Daughter

Unable to locate Riley, Jack Turner and his team escalates the operation.

They abduct Riley’s daughter Lauren.

She is taken to a classified black-site facility and held as leverage.

The message to Riley is clear:

Surrender… or she dies.

Slide 10 — Richard Sullivan

Richard Sullivan
Senior Security Operations executive at AISI.

For years, Sullivan has helped oversee the Truth Protocol.

But Riley’s escape forces him to question everything.

When Lauren is kidnapped to lure Riley into the open, Sullivan realizes the organization has crossed a line.

Instead of helping eliminate Riley…

Sullivan secretly decides to help him.

He helps Riley identify the black-site facility holding Lauren.

For the first time, someone inside the system turns against it.

Slide 11 — The Counterattack

Riley agrees to surrender. He will trade his life for Lauren’s.

But is that really his plan?

He and the remaining member of his old Special Forces team put together a plan to rescue Lauren.

The hunted becomes the hunter.

Slide 12 — The Final Mission

A high-risk tactical assault to rescue Lauren and destroy the program that started it all.

A mission complete with Blackhawk assault, fast roping to the objective, breeching and clearing, all in the name of brotherhood and duty.

Of course, in the real world, nothing ever goes as planned and not everyone makes it home.

But who is it?
